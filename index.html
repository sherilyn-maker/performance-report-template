🔍 Why Meta Ads Is Underperforming — Root Cause Analysis

#Root CauseEvidenceFix
1 Wrong conversion objective — optimizing for pixel form leads, but the business converts via phone Hotjar: 0.4% form conversion rate. CallRail: 531 phone calls in 12 months. Phone is the primary conversion for drain cleaning — no one fills a form for an emergency drain. The campaign is optimizing for the wrong action. Switch campaign objective to Calls (click-to-call) or optimize for phone call events. Set up CallRail as a conversion event in Meta pixel. Meta's algorithm will then find people likely to call, not fill forms.
2 Audience targeting too broad or wrong — reaching 29,508 people who don't need drain cleaning right now Drain cleaning is a demand-capture service (people search when they have a problem, not before). Interest-based Meta targeting shows ads to people who might own a home — not people with a clogged drain today. This is structurally mismatched to the product category. Meta Ads work better for this business with retargeting: show ads to people who visited the website or GBP profile in the last 30 days. These are warm leads who showed intent. Also test lookalike audiences from CallRail converters.
3 Landing page not optimized for paid traffic — clicks are going to a page that loses visitors Hotjar shows 86.2% of visitors are new, avg 192 seconds, mobile-dominant (73.6%). If Meta Ad clicks land on the homepage with no specific offer, the visitor has no reason to act immediately — they're comparing options and leave. Create a dedicated landing page for Meta Ad clicks: headline = "Las Vegas Drain Cleaning — $49 Special Today Only", large phone number, one CTA, no navigation. Hotjar this page specifically to watch the drop-off.
4 Ad creative likely not urgency-driven — service business ads need to trigger immediate action No creative data in the export, but 54,934 impressions generating only 3 leads implies a very low click-through and/or conversion rate. For a drain cleaning emergency service, "Is your drain clogged?" + price anchor + phone number beats any brand-awareness approach. Test direct-response creative: video of drain being cleared, "$49 drain cleaning today", large phone number overlay, Las Vegas location. Lead with the problem + instant solution, not brand imagery.
